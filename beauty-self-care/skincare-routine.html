Skincare Daily Ritual

Morning protection and evening repair, kept in disciplined rotation. Consistency is elegance

Morning

  1. Cleanse: Vanicream face wash
  2. Tone: Paula's Choice, as desired
  3. Vitamin C: SkinCeuticals C E Ferulic / generic
  4. Azelaic Acid: Anua 10% + Hyaluron
  5. Moisturise: Vanicream Enhanced Moisturizer
  6. Protect: Bioré Watery Sunscreen

Evening

  1. Cleanse: Cetaphil SA Exfoliating
  2. Treatment: see weekly rotation
  3. Repair: Snail Mucin
  4. Moisturise: Vanicream and/or AmLactin Lactic Acid 5%
  5. Seal: Vaseline on dry areas

AHA & Copper Peptides

Notes:
  • Copper peptides are reserved for non-retinoid evenings: keep them on separate nights from tretinoin
  • Benzoyl peroxide: use as a short-contact morning wash only
  • Tacrolimus: apply to irritated areas as needed
  • Adapalene may substitute tretinoin for gentler weeks
  • Ease up when the skin barrier asks for it

As Needed

  • Tacrolimus: spot treatment for irritated areas
  • Vaseline: slugging on dry zones
  • Benzoyl Peroxide: short-contact morning wash

Sebaceous Filaments

Nose & chin protocol

  • Gentle oil cleanser: to dissolve buildup
  • Niacinamide: to regulate sebum
  • Alternate tretinoin and Paula's Choice AHA
