Going-Out Makeup

A polished going-out routine with a soft base, structured contour, seasonal blush, and a clean end-of-night reset.

The Shopping List

  • Rare Beauty Pore Diffusing Primer
  • Armani Luminous Silk Foundation (1.5/5)
  • Fenty Contour Stick (Amber 01)
  • Morphe brow pencil
  • ColourPop powder eyeshadow, or cream shadow if lids are dry
  • Pen liner, mascara, lash curler.
  • Lip liner + lipstick + gloss stack.
  • Damp beauty blender and setting spray.

The Method

  1. Dampen the beauty blender in warm water and squeeze it out.
  2. Prime with Rare Beauty using a spatula or fingertips.
  3. Use one pump of Armani; spread with a spatula. Blend with the beauty blender, including neck. Add more only where needed.
  4. Contour below cheekbones, parallel to nose. Blend nose with fingers and cheek with a brush.
  5. Add blush (coral/orange-pink)
  6. Brows: lighter inner, darker tail. Eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara.
  7. Lip liner, lipstick, gloss. Setting spray, patted with the unused side of the blender.
Reset note: wash off with an eyelid scrub at the end of the night. Clean brushes often. Silk pillowcase, always.
