Terms & Conditions

Read in full before sending deposit · booking implies acceptance

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 7+ days in advance receive a full refund minus the deposit. Cancellations within 48–72 hours are charged 50% of the session rate. No-shows and same-day cancellations are charged in full.

Usage & Licensing

Casual and commercial sessions include personal and standard commercial usage rights. Extended licensing (advertising, billboards, mass distribution, OOH) requires a separate written agreement. All boudoir / lingerie images require my written approval before publication.

Editing & Delivery

Final selects are delivered via private online gallery within 10 business days. Rush delivery (5 days) available for $150. I reserve the right to decline portfolio inclusion if final editing falls below my standards.

Conduct & Safety

All sessions take place in a professional, respectful environment. I reserve the right to end any session immediately if boundaries are crossed, with no refund issued. A chaperone or assistant is welcome at any session.

Model Release

A model release is signed before the session begins. I retain rights to use select images for self-promotion and portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing. Boudoir / lingerie releases are separate and more restrictive no third-party distribution without my consent.

Travel & Location

Sessions within local city limits have no travel fee. Locations beyond 30km incur $0.55/km. Out-of-state or international bookings require travel, accommodation, and a minimum 2-session booking.

TFP Policy

TFP (trade-for-print) is offered case-by-case to published photographers and editorial teams aligned with my portfolio direction. Requires a portfolio link, concept brief, and intended publication. Not all requests are accepted. DM to inquire.

Payment & Refunds

Deposits are non-refundable. Balance refunds are processed within 14 business days when applicable. Disputes are resolved in good faith first; small-claims venue is the model's home jurisdiction. All amounts in USD unless agreed otherwise.
